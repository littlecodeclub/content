After School Coding Program

Join Our After School Programs through Canyon School District's Canyons Community Education 

Our program guides students through the fundamentals; of computers, computing science, game design and coding a micro bit computer. The experience is about problem solving through fun and discovery.
Students will program multiple types of games and a micro bit computer. They will take ownership of their own projects. By the end of the session, students will use a visual programming language to program a micro bit computer and create a game that they can share with their family and friends!

Some of the many coding concepts students will learn include: Sequencing, Logic, Conditionals, Loops, Variables, Cloning

All technology will be provided, students just need to come prepared to learn, code and create.
Students already familiar with visual coding will start with the advanced concepts of cloning and broadcasting.
